REVOTHSAVA

Revothsava is an inter-university fest organised by REVA University. Revothsava is a cultural, sports and management extravaganza! It is a platform for students of all academic backgrounds to come together and celebrate another academic year in their journey of students. It has an annual footfall of 20,000 students. It has a spectacular array of representation featuring some of the best talents from universities accross the country and a popular artist who has left the country spell-bound with his spectacular range of work.

It is an opportunity for students to create memories that last lifetime.Since its inception in 2012, Revothsava has grown not only in terms of student participation but also the quality and the quantity of events, media coverage and international recognition has reached unsurmountable heights. The aim is not only to nurture leadership qualities among students, but also to bring oneness among the student communities.

REVA University

REVA University was established in 2012 with the vision to install awareness, a zeal for higher learning and direct curiosity through education. The university offers 34 full-time under-graduate programmes, 24 full-time post-graduate programmes, 2 part-time post-graduate programme and 1 doctoral programme. The university has won various awards like Global India Education Award and Ranked no. 6 in Swachh Campus 2018 in higher education to name a few.

Recognised as one of the Top 50 private universities in India, REVA University, with innovative pedagogy, exemplary infrastructure, strives to benchmark new milestones in modern education. In less than two decades, the University has grown from a small community of 150 students to a vast institution that is educating and empowering 15,000+ students on campus with 2000 teaching faculties.
